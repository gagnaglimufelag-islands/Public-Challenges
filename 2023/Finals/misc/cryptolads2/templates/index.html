Input:

You will be given a base64-encoded ciphertext as input. The ciphertext represents the encrypted message, concealed by the AES encryption in Electronic Codebook (ECB) mode. You must devise an algorithm to decrypt this ciphertext without having access to the encryption key.

Output:

Your program should output the decrypted message as a string, revealing the hidden knowledge contained within the ciphertext.

Constraints:

  • The length of the ciphertext will be within a reasonable range for computation.
  • The decrypted message will be in English, consisting of alphabetic characters, digits, punctuation, and whitespace.
  • Please refrain from including any print statements in your code, as they may interfere with the evaluation process.
